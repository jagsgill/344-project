Instructions

START

This time you will have access to more information about each of the same 14 restaurants.

 

1. Once you hit start, you will swipe through 14 food photos choosing what you would like to eat.

    If you want to see more information, press the "More Info" button, or

    if you want to see more pictures, press the "More Pics" button.

    For each picture you can only pick one of these, the other option disappears! Choose carefully.

 

2. You must like at least 6 of them.

 

3. Your and your group members' selections will be used to find the restaurant(s) that best match your group.
