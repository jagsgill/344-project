Hello!

 

If this is your first time swiping for restaurants on Yelp, we have some tips for you below.

 

1. Once you hit start, you will swipe through 14 food photos choosing what you would like to eat.

    You must like at least 6 of them.

   

2. The heart at top left indicates how many people in your group have liked that photo so far.

 

3. Your and your group members' selections will be used to find the restaurant(s) that best match your group.
